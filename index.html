3: Buy and sell CCX tokens

Before you can buy and sell CCX tokens, you must create a trustline for CCX in your Stellar wallet. In the Starport wallet, click on the +Trustline button on the Balances screen. The asset code is CCX and the issuer domain is ccxtoken.github.io. Now you can trade CCX. Follow the link below to go directly to the StellarPort CCX <=> XLM exchange. If you find an acceptable offer, trade; otherwise, make your own offer.

4: Acquire CCX tokens

Use your Conceal wallet to send a CCX payment of the amount you want to deposit to the account: 

ccx7RRU14isYG6zAU7Lf2kSe5Pb5TU5ZL6cBLhenvjZsNeaNVC5XQ76jVASQQKjF8ceLfkLpX1nmHRfCQpBL5T8N2xenBfUkUr
Include a comment containing only your Stellar deposit address. Your Stellar deposit address is your Stella public key. The same amount of CCX tokens will be deposited to that Stellar account after eleven confirmations. Please remember that if you enter the wrong Stellar deposit address, you will lose your CCX.

5: Redeem CCX tokens for CCX

Register: You must first obtain a redemption code linked to a Conceal account. Do this by sending only that account's address as a comment in a Conceal payment of at least 1 CCX to the account: 

ccx7RRU14isYG6zAU7Lf2kSe5Pb5TU5ZL6cBLhenvjZsNeaNVC5XQ76jVASQQKjF8ceLfkLpX1nmHRfCQpBL5T8N2xenBfUkUr
After eleven confirmations you will receive a small payment with a comment containing a reusable code resembling CCX123456. Wait for a minute after the payment (with code) confirms before using the code. Widthraw: From your Stellar wallet, send CCX token payments to the account: 

GBXYZIMNU4HRJHJCG7ESQGZQUBKVG6BXMZXHI5N2WBXPYOVCOQ2Y2CCX
Include a text memo containing only your redemption code. Real CCX will be sent to the account linked to the code.

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

This is a new service, in beta mode. Use at your own risk. Split large transfers into a series of small transfers. Redeem CCX tokens for real CCX when not actively trading. This service may be discontinued after CCX becomes listed on a major exchange if trading volume drops. At this time, there is no deposit or withdrawal fee. Withdrawals are sometimes delayed but are usually quick.
